Max Financial Press is the publishing arm of Max Financial Group — a Miami-based CPA and advisory practice on Southeast Second Avenue. We exist for a specific reader: the entrepreneur, operator, or accredited investor who wants the arithmetic as much as the argument, and who has outgrown business books that mistake anecdote for analysis.

Every title in our catalogue is drawn from an active practice. That is the standard. Every playbook, entity diagram, and capital-stack schematic you will find on these pages has first been drafted at a client desk, redlined at a closing table, and reviewed on an IRS response. The books are instructional. But they are written in the register of a professional who is on the hook for the outcome.

We publish in English and in Spanish. Our Una Vida Max system runs to sixteen volumes and over 155,000 words, because the reader we are built for does business on both sides of the hemisphere — and should not have to read either translation twice to understand the other.

§ Author of the House David Hernández, CPA

A CPA who writes. Not a writer who quotes CPAs.

David Hernández is a Florida CPA and the founder of Brickell CPA, Inc. — a boutique advisory firm on Southeast Second Avenue serving entrepreneurs, accredited investors, and foreign clients across construction, real estate, healthcare, and logistics. He writes under the pen name Max L'Entrepreneur.

A Cuban immigrant who arrived in Miami in December 1997, David holds a DBA in Entrepreneurship and has taught at FIU, Miami Dade College, and Millennia Atlantic. His books are drawn from live engagements — forensic files, IRS responses, exit transactions, construction-loan portfolios — the working papers that never make it into airport business titles.
